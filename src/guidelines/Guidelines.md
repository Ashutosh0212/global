Primary Page Background (The "Sky" Effect)

Visual: A soft, vertical gradient that starts as a pale blue at the top and fades to white at the bottom. This creates an open, airy feel.

CSS / Tailwind: bg-gradient-to-b from-blue-50 to-white

Hex: Approx #eff6ff fading to #ffffff.

Emphasis Section Background (The "Deep Ocean" Effect)

Visual: A solid, vibrant medium-blue used for specific sections like "Why Travel with Global Travels" to make white text pop.

CSS / Tailwind: bg-blue-500 or bg-[#2596be] (a specific tropical blue).

Content Surface

Visual: Clean white cards or transparent backgrounds floating over the gradient.

CSS / Tailwind: bg-white or bg-white/80 (semi-transparent).
